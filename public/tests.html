Label Check Approach & test cases

How it works, and where it stops

AI reads the label and weighs the rare close calls; deterministic code checks what the law states exactly (the government warning, ABV, proof, net contents); the agent decides every case. Full approach, tools, and assumptions live in the repo: README.md and ASSUMPTIONS.md, with the reproducible model comparison in docs/model-eval.md.

Known limits, shown not hidden
